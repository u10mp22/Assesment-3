Inverurie Bowling Center is fun for all the family! With both ten pin bowling and bowling on the green available there is something for everyone! Also available is a wide selection of food and drink, and other entertainment such as pool tables and air hockey! Visit This website for more details and booking.













Garioch Heritage Center is the place to go if you want to find out more about Inveruries vast history. There are many artifacts on display that represent many different periods of Inveruries History. There is also a cafe that serves delicious home bakes and other foods. The Heritage Center runs many events throught the year so make sure to check out This website for more information.





Skyline Trampoline Park is one for the kids! With plenty to offer including groups, clubs or just normal trampolining sessions. There is also a cafe that serves bugers, milkshakes and much more. Visit This website for more details and booking.
